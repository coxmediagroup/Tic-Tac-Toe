How to Play Tic-Tac-Toe

Tic-Tac-Toe is a simple two-player game played on a 3x3 grid. The object of Tic-Tac-Toe is to make a horizontal, vertical, or diagonal row of three Xs or Os. The player and AI (Artificial Intelligence) alternate turns, each trying to block the other while completing a row. There are eight possible rows to win the game, but if both players play their best then each game will end in a tie.

Tic-Tac-Toe Tips

  • The middle space is the most valuable, with 4 possible rows
  • Corner spaces are better than side spaces
  • Watch for opponent making two threats to win
  • If opponent selects diagonal corners and you have the middle space, selecting another corner will result in a loss
